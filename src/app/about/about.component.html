Signing Up

If you are new to Ladder Ranking, please sign up by clicking on button.

  • Enter your complete details in the signup form (Full Name).

  • We have made following 5 categories for all 4 sports. Different categories have different starting point distribution. Choose the one most suitable for you

    • Beginner - 200 points
    • NSO - 300 points
    • Intermediate - 500 points
    • Inter IIT Camp - 700 points
    • Inter IIT Team - 800 points

    Secys have the authority to change your category

If you have already registered, then login using button and start playing!

Ladder Table
  • View the Ladder Table by clicking the button.

  • Select Your Sport from the selector in the top left corner of the screen.

  • Select Your Gender from the selector in the top left corner of the screen.

  • You can also filter the table by searching in the search bar.

  • Clicking on any player will give you their overall profile and you can challenge them by pressing the Challenge button.

You can only challenge in the sport you have selected from drop-down box!

Accepting Challenges
  • Challenges Sent and Received can be seen from the Tab

  • You can see the date and time of a challenge by tapping on the challenge and can also accept and reject from there only.

  • You can also delete a challenge sent by clicking Undo button.

  • If your challenge has been accepted by the opponent, it will to green. If it has been rejected, it would be red.

Updating Result
  • After the match has been concluded, you must update the result. To do so, go to the tab and click on the match for which you want to update the result.

  • Entering Score is little tricky. But pay attention and you would be fine.

  • Match Score: (Number of sets you have won) - (Number of sets opponent has won)

    Eg. 3-2


  • Set Score: (Number of points you have in each set) - (Number of points opponent has in each set)

    Eg. 11-9 11-3 4-11 6-11 11-9

    (Separated by space)

  • After this, you would be redirected to the Recent Matches page. Here, all matches are color-coded for your convenience.
